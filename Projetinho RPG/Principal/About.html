An unspecific project about an rpg based on my personal tastes and that works only in imagination!

Basically I love fictional stories, fantastic and unimaginable in our current world. Reality can be very chaotic at times so running away from it feels enchanting. Since I was little I'm fascinated by stories and the immersion present in Rpgs, I developed this passion and for this reason I decided to use it as a template for this page. I think a lot of people can relate since the Mmos are packed with players. Each with their individuality and their own perspective starting a fantastic journey! It even seems like a dream.
